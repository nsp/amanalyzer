What it's all about

This project is just a collection of scripts I threw together to visualize Dan Harmon's AMA. I enjoyed every one of his answers, and was also impressed by how thoroughly and quickly he replied. The response where he cried for the second time was posted less than 15 minutes after the previous comment. Here's what I've come up with.

Depends on praw, tested with Python 2.6.6, charts drawn with Google Chart Tools
